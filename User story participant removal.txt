User story name: 
REQ-10 | Participant removal by Administrator

As an Administrator, I want to remove participant from the room until the randomization starts so that I can manage and keep participants list accurate and up to date.

Acceptance criteria:
• The Administrator can remove any participant from the room until the randomization starts.
• Removed participant lose access to the room and their data is deleted.
• The system updates the number of participants in the room accordingly.

AC: Participant removal
Given the Admin is in the room,
When the Admin performs the ‘Remove participant’ action,
Then the system:
• Identifies the chosen participant,
• Removes participant from the room,
• Revokes the access to the room of the chosen participant,
• Updates the participants list and count,
• Deletes data of the chosen participant.
